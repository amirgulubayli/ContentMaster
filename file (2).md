## 🎬 MEDIA & AI RULES (UPDATED)

### ✅ Gemini API (Images Only)
- **Use Gemini for image generation** (graphics, memes, carousels, backgrounds)
- **NO Gemini video generation** (overkill, not desired)

### ✅ Video Creation Policy
- Videos built **manually via Remotion + ffmpeg**
- Video style = **meme formats** or **image-based fades** (fade in/out, slideshow)
- **No AI-generated human videos**
- **No AI influencers speaking on camera**

### ✅ Persona Rules
- **AI personas allowed** for static images / lifestyle photos
- **If a persona’s face is shown, it must be MALE**
- **Female accounts are allowed**, but **must not show a female face**
- No female “AI influencer” type content
